Omelette

Description

A quick and easy recipe for a delicious omelette full of ham, cheese, onions, peppers and tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • 4 whole eggs
  • 3 slices of ham
  • Red onion
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • 2 campari tomatoes
  • 1 half bell pepper
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Put pan on stove over medium low heat with a quick spray of Pam
  2. Dice onions, tomatoes, and peppers
  3. Put veges into pan to sautee for 5 minutes
  4. Cut ham into squares
  5. Crack eggs into bowl and whisk
  6. When soft, remove veges from pan
  7. Respray pan and add eggs to pan
  8. After about 3-4 minutes, flip eggs
  9. Place cheese on one side ofeggs, place veges and ham on top
  10. Fold other half of eggs over
  11. Remove from pan and enjoy!
