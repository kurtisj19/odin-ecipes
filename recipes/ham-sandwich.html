Ham Sandwich

Description

An easy and scrumptious ham sandwich to enjoy during your lunch break or on the go.

Ingredients

  • Two slices of your choice of bread
  • Mayonaise
  • Mustard
  • Tomato
  • Pickles
  • Ham
  • Cheese
  • Iceburg lettuce

Steps

  1. Place bread on plate, spread mayo on one slice, mustard on the other
  2. Cut three slices of tomato, or however many you want
  3. Place 2-3 pieces of lettuce on bread
  4. Layer ham on lettuce
  5. Add tomatoes and pickles
  6. Place other piece of bread on top and enjoy!
