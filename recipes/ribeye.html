Ribeye

Description

A mouthwatering and juicy pan-seared ribeye packed with beefy flavor.

Ingredients

  • Ribeye steak
  • Butter
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder

Steps

  1. Salt both sides of ribeye steak and let rest at room temperature
  2. Place pan on stove over high heat. Add 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  3. After pan is hot, gently lay steak in pan
  4. Flip steak every 2 min for four times
  5. Add pepper and garlic powder to both sides of steak after first two flips
  6. Add knob of butter to pan
  7. Baste steak with melted butter for 2 minutes
  8. Remove steak from pan and let rest for 1 minutes
  9. Enjoy!
